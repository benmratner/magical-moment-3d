Home

 

Magical Moment 3D Imaging is an independently owned and operated service, providing expectant parents an opportunity to see their unborn child via true-to-life images generated by the latest in 3D ultrasound technology. 

We believe that real-time, high-quality images can jump-start the formation of that lifelong bond between parents and their baby. Unlike with traditional 2D ultrasound imaging, our service provides parents with an astonishingly life-like representation of their child's face. Even more, we can share with you, in real-time, the experience of seeing your baby yawn, cry, smile, swallow & even blink for the very first time! 

 

It’s with our full appreciation of pregnancy's magic that we're so excited to offer your family a head-start opportunity to begin bonding with your unborn child in such a way previously deemed impossible! We know you just can't wait to start filling out that photo album of memories, dedicated to your bundle of joy. Why wait any longer?! Pregnancy is certainly a momentous occasion, so don't hesitate to capture that magical moment. We know you'll cherish it forever!

 

HOW MAGICAL MOMENT 3D IMAGING WORKS:

  • We are a mobile ultrasound service 
  • Schedule your appointment in advance and we will arrive at your chosen location
  • We come to you fully equipped and with your assistance we will determine the best and most comfortable area to set up your ultrasound
  • Our state of the art ultrasound machine is the size of a laptop so you don’t have to worry about finding a large space in your home
  • We can connect our machine to your home’s big screen TV and in no time you and your loved ones will see your baby in real-time
  • Feel free to invite as many people as you want to share this amazing experience with you
  • We will even come to your gender reveal party or baby shower!

 


*** For those couples that prefer to leave the child's gender as a birthday surprise, we work very carefully to maintain that secret while still providing you with the best 3D/4D ultrasound experience possible. We understand the desire to “peek”, while preserving the element of surprise! ***
